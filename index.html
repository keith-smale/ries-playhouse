Welcome to Rie's Playhouse

Hi! I'm Marie, welcome to Rie's Playhouse.

Thank you for taking the time to look at my website.

I am an Ofsted Registered childminder with a level 3 qualification in home based childcare, located in Eastwood, Essex. I have all the necessary insurance and first aid training in order to provide a safe environment for children.

I live with my husband and two children, Madison and Joshua and our cats Popsy and Suki. I am friendly, patient and resilient. I have a high focus on child-initiated activites and will always strive to get the best out of every child. I am passionate about children and their wellbeing; you can be assured that each and every child in my care will be treated equally, fairly and as a unique individual.

I am committed to delivering the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) and maintaining a warm and safe environment, in which all children are given the opportunity to flourish.

Availability

I am available all year round, Monday to Friday and between the hours 8am to 6pm. I do not usually work on public holidays, however special arrangements can be discussed.

I offer the school run to Eastwood Primary and can also go via Eastwood Academy if required.

For more information or to enquire about a space for your child, please contact me.

Fees

Please find the details of my fees below. Fees are charged per child and are invoiced monthly in advance.

Government funded places for 2, 3 and 4 year olds and Childcare Vouchers are also accepted.

  • Childcare Fees
  • Standard Rate £5.50 per hour
  • Additional Charges
  • Late Collection Fee £2.75 per 15 mins late
  • Late Payment Fee £5.00 per day

Parents' holiday incurs a 50% charge of the Standard Rate for the duration as per your usual agreed hours where
4 weeks notice is given. Holiday with less than 4 weeks notice will be charged at the full Standard Rate for the duration as per your usual agreed hours.

There will be no charges for any holiday I take.
